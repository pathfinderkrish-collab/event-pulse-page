Inter-College Football League 2025

About this Event

Battle for the trophy! ⚽ Watch teams from across colleges compete in the annual Football League. Experience a week full of intense action, incredible teamwork, and campus spirit. Come cheer for your college and witness the crowning of the new champions.

Tournament Format

Group Stages: 16 teams compete in 4 groups.
Knockout Rounds: Top 2 teams from each group advance.
Semi-Finals: The final four battle for a spot in the final.
The Grand Final: One winner takes all.
