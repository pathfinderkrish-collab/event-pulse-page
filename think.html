Design Thinking Workshop 2025

About this Workshop

Boost your creative problem-solving skills! This hands-on workshop introduces the full Design Thinking process, from understanding user needs to building and testing prototypes.

This workshop is interactive. You will work in groups to solve a real-world campus challenge.

Workshop Modules

1. Empathize Learn to conduct user interviews to find pain points.
2. Define & Ideate Brainstorm and cluster ideas to define a problem.
3. Prototype Build a low-fidelity solution (paper or digital).
4. Test Get rapid feedback on your prototype from peers.
