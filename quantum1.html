About this Expo

Explore the future of computation at the Quantum Tech Expo. This event brings together researchers, industry leaders, and students to explore quantum computing, cryptography, and quantum-based AI.

The Quantum Tech Expo is the premier event for anyone interested in the next generation of technology. Our mission is to demystify quantum science and demonstrate its potential impact on AI, medicine, and security.

What to Expect

Live Demos See real quantum prototypes in action.
Expert Panels Hear from leading physicists and engineers.
Project Showcase Explore student projects on quantum algorithms.
Networking Meetups for students, faculty, and recruiters.
